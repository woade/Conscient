The Brand

Precision-cut sportswear for the unapologetic.

Conscient started with a simple frustration: gymwear that looked the same as everything else on the rack. We took the cut and construction of vintage athletic wear and pushed the finish somewhere sharper — chrome-treated fabrics, saturated crimson, and silhouettes built to hold their shape.

Every piece is numbered and produced in limited runs, cut from heavyweight cotton and finished with the kind of detail that only shows up when you're paying attention. Conscient isn't loud. It's built for people who don't need it to be.
